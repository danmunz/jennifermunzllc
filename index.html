Services

I'm focused on getting great people into government and keeping them there. Whether you're looking to start a career in public service or ascend into the Senior Executive Service, I can help you navigate the process and prepare for success.

Navigating Federal Hiring

Whether it's your first time thinking about federal employment, you’ve been trying to get "in" for years, or you're a current fed looking for the next rung in your career ladder. We’ll talk about your goals, assess your qualifications, find the right type of federal job for you, and create a plan to get you there.

Federal Resume Consultation

Writing a federal resume is a unique process, and without a strong federal-style resume you won't get in the door. Based on your career path and what kinds of jobs you’re interested in, I will help you create an outstanding federal resume that will help get you the interview.

Senior Executive Service Prep

If you’re considering applying to SES jobs, having trouble getting interviews, or just want to take your application package from good to great, I will help you prepare a strong, authentic resume and ECQ package. My mock interview process will prepare you for behavior-based and structured ECQ interviews that are often part of the SES selection process.

Vacancy Discovery

Navigating USAJobs can be confusing and complex. I’ll explain how USAJobs works and help you understand the different types of positions, application methods, and job requirements you see listed there. We’ll also talk about your background and experience to make sure you’re applying for the right jobs.

Interested but not sure what to do next? Let’s talk! I’ll give you a complimentary overview of my services, answer your questions, and tailor a package that's right for you.

About Jennifer Munz

Connect on:

Jennifer Munz is a federal human resources expert and manager with a decade of experience helping organizations recruit, train, and retain top talent.

Jennifer specializes in senior executive positions. She has worked in federal human resources for ten years, leading teams in recruiting, job classification, performance management, political employment, and executive development. She has a unique perspective, having worked in large and small agencies with diverse missions and workforce profiles.

She holds a Bachelor of Arts degree in Political Science from Auburn University and a Master’s Degree in Organizational Management from The George Washington University.

Jennifer understands what kind of talent federal agencies are looking for, and how to position yourself to be the strongest possible candidate. Let her help you.
